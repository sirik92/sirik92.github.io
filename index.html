WHO WE ARE

Val Company, established in 2024, is one of Iraq's fastest-growing cement firms, known for its quality, speed, and professionalism. It operates as a proud part of Baroque Company, a leading construction and contracting firm founded in 2015.

Both companies are owned and led by Mr. Dawan Omar Salih, whose vision, expertise, and commitment to excellence have shaped their growth and success.
