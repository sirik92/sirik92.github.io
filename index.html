Our Products

Ordinary Portland Cement (OPC)

Ordinary Portland Cement (OPC) is the most reliable and widely used binding agent in the construction industry. It is manufactured in strict accordance with international standards, such as ASTM C150 (Type I) or EN 197-1, ensuring superior performance across diverse structural and architectural applications.

Read More

Technical Specifications & Features: * Structural Strength: Characterized by a high rate of compressive strength development, making it ideal for structural elements subjected to heavy loads. * Setting Time: Engineered with balanced initial and final setting times, providing engineers with the necessary flexibility for mixing, transportation, and placement. * Durability: Offers excellent resistance to weathering and environmental factors, ensuring the long-term integrity of concrete structures. * Compatibility: Highly compatible with various chemical admixtures to enhance workability, flow, or water reduction. Product Applications: * All reinforced concrete works, including bridges, columns, and slabs. * Ready-mix concrete production. * Precast concrete units and masonry products. * High-quality finishing works and cement mortar.

Super Block Cement

Super Block Cement is a specialized, high-performance product engineered specifically for the masonry and precast industry.

Read More

Its unique formulation is optimized for automatic and semi-automatic production lines, ensuring exceptional quality and cost-efficiency. Technical Specifications & Features: * High Early Strength: Developed to provide rapid hardening, allowing for faster demolding and increased daily production turnover. * Reduced Wastage: Exceptional bonding properties significantly minimize breakage and crumbling during handling and transportation. * Fine Finishes: The optimized fineness ensures smooth surfaces, sharp edges, and uniform textures for blocks and curbstones. * Mix Efficiency: Provides excellent coating of aggregates, resulting in a consistent and homogeneous mix with optimized cement content. Product Applications: * Production of all types of masonry blocks (Hollow, Solid, and Insulated). * Manufacturing of Interlocking tiles and paving stones. * Production of Curbstones and unreinforced precast elements. * All cement-based products requiring short curing cycles.

Bagged Ordinary Portland Cement (OPC)

Our Bagged OPC is the gold standard for flexibility and reliability in the construction sector.

Read More

Packed under stringent quality control measures, it ensures that the cement retains its full chemical and physical integrity from the production line to the project site. Technical Specifications & Features: * Premium Packaging: Encased in high-strength, multi-ply, moisture-resistant paper or laminated PP bags, designed to withstand rigorous handling and extend shelf life. * Consistency: Delivers uniform fineness and color across every bag, ensuring consistent performance for concrete and mortar mixes. * Operational Flexibility: The ideal solution for medium-to-small scale projects and sites with restricted access for bulk tankers. * High Workability: Engineered for optimal setting times and high early strength, facilitating both manual and mechanical mixing processes. Product Applications: * General reinforced concrete structures (Slabs, Columns, Foundations). * Masonry, plastering, and tiling mortars. * Renovation, repair, and maintenance projects. * General infrastructure and pavement works.

Bagged Portland Composite Cement (CEM II)

CEM II is an advanced structural cement engineered by inter-grinding Portland clinker with carefully selected mineral additives or pozzolanas.

Read More

It offers a perfect synergy between high durability, superior workability, and eco-friendly construction. Technical Specifications & Features: * Superior Workability: Its fine particle distribution ensures a smoother mix, making it easier to handle and reducing voids and surface cracks. * Low Heat of Hydration: Produces less heat during the setting process, making it ideal for hot weather concreting and minimizing thermal cracking. * Enhanced Durability: The composite components provide increased resistance to chemical attacks and reduce permeability, protecting the embedded reinforcement. * Premium Packaging: Supplied in moisture-proof, high-durability bags to maintain optimal freshness and performance during storage. Product Applications: * Internal and external plastering (rendering) and architectural finishes. * Masonry works, bricklaying, and tile adhesives. * Mass concrete and non-structural elements. * General paving and floor screeds.
